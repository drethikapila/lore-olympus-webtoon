Major Characters in Lore Olympus Webtoon

Hades

Hades, the God of the underworld, takes center stage. Often depicted as a handsome blue man in a business suit, he runs the Underworld Corporation.

Persephone

Persephone, the goddess of spring, is the story’s heroine alongside the underworld. Initially portrayed as an innocent young woman, her character develops dramatically.

Zeus

Zeus, the king of the gods, is depicted as a yellow-skinned man with long hair known for his promiscuity and egotism.

Poseidon

Poseidon, the God of the sea, is characterized by a carefree temperament and loyalty to his family.

Eros

Eros, the God of love, is a strong figure and supportive friend to Persephone.

Minor Lore Olympus Characters

Hermes

Hermes, the God of speed travel, is depicted as an athletic man in red. He works as a soul collector for Hades and is an old friend of Persephone.

Apollo

Apollo, the sun God, becomes an essential antagonist after Persephone is raped, bringing severe issues of power abuse and trauma into play.

Artemis

Artemis, the goddess of the hunt, is Persephone’s best friend and roommate. She is fiercely protective of Persephone.
